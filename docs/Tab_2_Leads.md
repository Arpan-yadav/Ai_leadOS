# Tab 2: Leads

## 1. Purpose and Role
The Leads tab serves as the central directory for all potential customers. Its primary functionality is data management—allowing users to view, add, edit, filter, and organize the contact information of people they want to sell to. It acts as the foundational database that feeds into every other part of the system (like Sequences and the Pipeline).

## 2. Proposed Alternative Naming Conventions
- **Contact Directory** (Clear and universally understood)
- **Lead Database** (Highlights its function as a storage hub)
- **Prospects** (Standard sales terminology)

## 3. Key Components and Features

### The Leads Data Table
- **What it is:** The core component of the page, displaying a structured list of leads.
- **Components:** Columns for Name, Company, Source, Score, Status, and Actions.
- **Use Case:** Users scan this table to quickly find specific contacts, see their current status (e.g., "New", "Engaged"), and check their AI-assigned Lead Score. 

### "New Lead" Button & Modal
- **What it is:** A prominent button at the top that opens a form to add a person to the database.
- **Components:** Input fields for the lead's name, company, and contact details.
- **Use Case:** Used by sales reps after a networking event or a cold call to manually enter a new prospect into the system.

### Bulk Selection & Deletion
- **What it is:** Checkboxes next to each lead's name.
- **Components:** When one or more checkboxes are clicked, a red "Delete Selected" button appears.
- **Use Case:** Allows users to easily clean up their database by removing junk leads or contacts that are no longer relevant in one swift action.

### Export to CSV Button
- **What it is:** A utility button to download data.
- **Components:** Generates a spreadsheet file of the currently viewed leads.
- **Use Case:** Used when a manager needs to create an external report, back up data, or share the contact list with someone who doesn't have access to the platform.

### AI Intelligence Selection Panel (Row Details)
- **What it is:** A detailed view that appears when a user clicks on a specific lead row.
- **Components:** Shows deep insights gathered by the AI about that specific person, including recommended talking points.
- **Use Case:** Before jumping on a sales call, a rep will look at this panel to understand the lead's background and tailor their pitch perfectly.

## 4. Navigation & Links to Other Pages
- **To Sequences:** Leads managed here are often selected and pushed into the **Sequences** tab for automated email campaigns.
- **To Pipeline:** Once a lead responds positively and is qualified, they are conceptually (and sometimes systematically) moved to the **Pipeline** tab to be tracked as an active deal.
