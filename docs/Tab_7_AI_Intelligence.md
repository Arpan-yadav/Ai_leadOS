# Tab 7: AI Intelligence

## 1. Purpose and Role
The AI Intelligence tab is the "Brain" of the platform. It is a dedicated space where the user interacts directly with the system's underlying artificial intelligence. Its role is to analyze massive amounts of data from across all the other tabs and distill it down into actionable insights, scoring, and predictions that a human could never calculate manually.

## 2. Proposed Alternative Naming Conventions
- **AI Brain** (Simple, punchy, and clear)
- **Strategic Insights** (Focuses on the business value provided)
- **Copilot** (Frames the AI as a helpful assistant)

## 3. Key Components and Features

### Copilot Focus
- **What it is:** A highlighted panel that points the user's attention to urgent matters.
- **Components:** Alerts like "3 High-intent opportunities detected."
- **Use Case:** When a user is overwhelmed, they look here to figure out exactly who they should call first to maximize their chances of closing a deal today.

### Deep Scanning Engine
- **What it is:** The visual representation of the AI analyzing data.
- **Components:** Often shown as a scanning animation or progress bar across the database.
- **Use Case:** Provides transparency, letting the user know that the AI is actively reading emails, checking lead scores, and updating its predictions in real-time.

### Predictive Scoring Models
- **What it is:** The mathematical explanation behind the "Lead Scores".
- **Components:** Breakdowns showing *why* a lead is scored 95/100 (e.g., "+20 for opening 3 emails, +15 for company size").
- **Use Case:** Builds trust. Sales reps are more likely to trust the AI's recommendations if they can see the logic behind why a specific lead was flagged as "Hot".

## 4. Navigation & Links to Other Pages
- **To Leads & Pipeline:** The intelligence generated here is injected into the **Leads** table and the **Pipeline** cards as "Scores". 
- **From Analytics:** The AI consumes the raw mathematical data generated in the **Analytics** tab to form its insights.
