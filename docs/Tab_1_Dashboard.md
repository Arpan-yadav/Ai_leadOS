# Tab 1: Dashboard

## 1. Purpose and Role
The Dashboard is the "Command Center" of AI LeadOS. Its primary role is to provide a high-level, real-time snapshot of the entire business at a single glance. Users log in and immediately see the health of their sales pipeline, their most urgent priorities, and the overall performance metrics without needing to dig through complex data tables. 

## 2. Proposed Alternative Naming Conventions
- **Command Center** (Conveys control and a high-level overview)
- **Global Overview** (Simple and professional)
- **Home Base** (Friendly and welcoming)

## 3. Key Components and Features

### Global Intelligence Metrics
- **What it is:** A row of crucial numbers at the very top of the page.
- **Components:** Total Revenue, Active Leads, Conversion Rate, and Pipeline Value. 
- **Use Case:** Gives managers and executives instant answers to "How are we doing today?". The green/red percentage indicators show if these metrics are trending up or down compared to last week.

### Intelligence Performance Chart
- **What it is:** A large visual graph in the center of the screen.
- **Components:** A glowing curve chart plotting Revenue and Leads over the days of the week. Hovering over the graph shows exact numbers for a specific day.
- **Use Case:** Helps users spot trends. For example, if "Leads" spike on Wednesdays but "Revenue" drops on Thursdays, the team can investigate why.

### Lead Origin Map
- **What it is:** A breakdown of where leads are coming from.
- **Components:** Progress bars representing different channels (WhatsApp, Email, Meta Ads, LinkedIn, Referral) with their respective percentages.
- **Use Case:** Tells the marketing team which platforms are working best so they know where to spend their advertising budget.

### Strategic Recommendations (AI Panel)
- **What it is:** A specialized widget driven by the AI engine.
- **Components:** Displays actionable alerts like "High Intent Detected".
- **Use Case:** Instead of just showing data, the AI tells the user *what to do next*. This is where the platform acts like an assistant rather than just a database.

### Intelligence Log
- **What it is:** A real-time feed of recent activity.
- **Components:** Shows entries like "James Wilson from TechCorp recently sourced from LinkedIn".
- **Use Case:** Provides transparency into the latest events happening in the system right now.

## 4. Navigation & Links to Other Pages
The Dashboard acts as a launching pad. While it doesn't have many direct interactive buttons that edit data, the insights provided here tell the user where to go next:
- If Active Leads are dropping, the user will navigate to the **Leads** or **Sequences** tab to generate more.
- If Pipeline Value is high, the user will go to the **Pipeline** tab to try and close those deals.
