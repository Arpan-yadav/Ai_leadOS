# Tab 3: Pipeline

## 1. Purpose and Role
The Pipeline tab is the execution engine of the sales team. It visualizes the journey of a "Deal" (a qualified lead) from initial contact to the final sale. Its role is to help sales representatives and managers track exactly where money is sitting in the sales funnel and identify any bottlenecks preventing deals from closing.

## 2. Proposed Alternative Naming Conventions
- **Deal Board** (Action-oriented and descriptive)
- **Sales Funnel** (Classic business terminology)
- **Opportunities** (Focuses on the potential value)

## 3. Key Components and Features

### Kanban Board Layout
- **What it is:** A visual, column-based board (similar to Trello).
- **Components:** Vertical columns representing stages like "Lead", "Contacted", "Discovery", "Proposal", "Negotiation", "Won", and "Lost".
- **Use Case:** Provides an immediate, visual understanding of the sales process. If the "Proposal" column is overflowing, the team knows they need to focus on following up and closing.

### Deal Cards & Drag-and-Drop
- **What it is:** Interactive cards representing individual deals.
- **Components:** Each card shows the company name, the deal value (e.g., $15,000), and a visual indicator of the lead's score/health.
- **Use Case:** As a sales rep makes progress (e.g., they just finished a discovery call), they literally drag the card from "Contacted" and drop it into "Discovery". This updates the database instantly and gives a satisfying sense of progress.

### Column Value Summaries
- **What it is:** Dynamic numbers at the top of each column.
- **Components:** Calculates the total dollar amount of all the cards currently sitting in that specific stage.
- **Use Case:** Allows a manager to say, "We have $50,000 sitting in the Proposal stage right now. If we close half, we hit our monthly goal."

### Search and Filtering (Upcoming/Planned)
- **What it is:** The search bar at the top of the interface.
- **Components:** Filters the board to only show specific deals.
- **Use Case:** A rep wants to only see deals assigned to them, or deals worth more than $10,000. 

## 4. Navigation & Links to Other Pages
- **From Leads:** The Pipeline is the natural next step after the **Leads** tab. Once a lead shows purchasing intent, they become a card on this board.
- **To Tasks:** Managing deals generates work. Looking at a card in the Pipeline might prompt a user to switch to the **Tasks** tab to create a reminder to "Send contract to TechCorp."
- **To Analytics:** The movement of cards from left to right generates the "Stage Conversion" metrics viewed in the **Analytics** tab.
